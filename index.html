Hello! My name is
Michael Korsh.

I'm a Data Reporter on the investigations team at the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

I received my master's and undergraduate degrees from Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism. A recipient of the Medill Valedictorian Award and a host of other student honors, I spent the past year working on The Washington Post's award-winning Pandora Papers investigation in collaboration with the Medill Investigative Lab in Washington, D.C. The series exposed corruption and unethical activities of world leaders, corporate executives and others who hid billions of dollars in secret offshore accounts. I also worked on the Post's national investigation in 2020 of safety failures and deaths in nursing homes during the COVID-19 pandemic.
